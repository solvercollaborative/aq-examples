Members Area

This page demonstrates the difference between public and authenticated AQ chatbot modes. Toggle the switch below to simulate logging in.

Public Mode
Public Mode: The chatbot can answer general questions about courses, schedule, and location. It does not have access to member-specific information like the directory or account details.

To access member features, log in with your Lakeside Sailing account. Members get access to the member directory, course history, and personalized schedule information through the chatbot.

Authenticated Mode: The chatbot now has access to member-specific content. Try asking "Is Maria Chen a member?" or "What courses has James Rivera taught?"

How Authentication Works

In a real integration, your website's login system generates a signed JWT token and passes it to the AQ widget. This token tells AQ who the user is and what content they're authorized to see.
