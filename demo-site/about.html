About Lakeside Sailing Academy

Founded in 2008 by Captain Maria Chen, Lakeside Sailing Academy has grown from a two-boat operation into Chicago's largest community sailing school. We're based at Burnham Harbor on Lake Michigan's southwestern shore.

Our mission is to make sailing accessible, safe, and fun for everyone. We believe time on the water builds confidence, teaches problem-solving, and creates lifelong friendships.
