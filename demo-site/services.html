Our Courses

We offer US Sailing certified courses from absolute beginner through advanced racing. All courses include boat usage, safety equipment, and a course completion certificate.

Basic Keelboat (BK)

Learn the fundamentals: rigging, points of sail, tacking, jibing, and docking. No experience needed. 4 sessions over 2 weekends.

$595

Basic Cruising (BC)

Build on BK skills with navigation, anchoring, heavy weather techniques, and overnight trip planning. Prerequisite: BK.

$795

Bareboat Cruising (BBC)

Advanced coastal navigation, passage planning, crew management, and systems troubleshooting. Prerequisite: BC.

$995

Racing Fundamentals

Starting sequences, mark rounding, tactics, and rules of racing. Includes 3 practice races. Prerequisite: BK.

$695

Youth Dinghy (ages 10-16)

Kids learn to sail Optimist and Laser dinghies in a fun, safe environment. Weekly sessions, June through August.

$450

Private Lessons

One-on-one instruction tailored to your goals. Available for any skill level. 2-hour sessions on your schedule.

$175/session
