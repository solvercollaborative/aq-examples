Why Lakeside?

Founded in 2008, Lakeside Sailing Academy is Chicago's premier sailing school. We offer US Sailing certified instruction on Lake Michigan with a fleet of well-maintained keelboats and dinghies.

Certified Instructors

All instructors hold US Sailing certifications and have 5+ years of teaching experience on the Great Lakes.

Modern Fleet

Train on our fleet of J/22s, Colgate 26s, and Laser dinghies — maintained to the highest standards.

Flexible Scheduling

Weekend, weekday evening, and intensive week-long course formats to fit your schedule.

2026 Season

Registration is now open for our summer sailing season. Classes run May through September with sessions starting every two weeks.

New this year: advanced racing clinics and overnight cruising courses to Milwaukee and Michigan City.
