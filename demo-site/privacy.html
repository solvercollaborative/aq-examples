Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026. Lakeside Sailing Academy is a fictional business used to demonstrate the AnswerQuestions AI Guide. This notice explains what happens to data when you use the chatbot on this demo site.

What we process

  • Your chatbot messages. Questions you type into the AI Guide are sent to the AnswerQuestions service to generate an answer from this site's public content. Please don't enter real personal or sensitive information — this is a public demonstration.
  • Basic request data. Standard technical details (such as IP address and browser type) used to serve the site and protect it from abuse.

Who it's shared with

  • AnswerQuestions / Solver Collaborative — operates the AI Guide that answers your questions.
  • OpenAI — the AI Guide uses OpenAI's models to compose answers from retrieved content.
  • Cloudflare — we use Cloudflare Turnstile to confirm that visitors are human and shield the demo from automated abuse. Turnstile may process information about your browser and network to make that assessment, as described in Cloudflare's Turnstile Privacy Addendum and Privacy Policy.

What we don't do

We don't sell your data or use it for advertising, and because this is a demonstration we don't retain chat content beyond what's needed to operate and improve the demo.

Contact

Questions about this demo? Reach Solver Collaborative at answerquestions.ai.
