Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026. This is an independent demonstration of the AnswerQuestions AI Guide, built by Solver Collaborative — not affiliated with or endorsed by Maven. This notice explains what happens to data when you use the chatbot on this demo.

What we process

  • Your chatbot messages. Questions you type are sent to the AnswerQuestions service to generate an answer from public content. Please don't enter real personal or sensitive information — this is a public demo.
  • Basic request data. Standard technical details (IP address, browser type) used to serve the site and protect it from abuse.

Who it's shared with

  • AnswerQuestions / Solver Collaborative — operates the AI Guide.
  • OpenAI — used to compose answers from retrieved content.
  • Cloudflare — Cloudflare Turnstile confirms that visitors are human and shields the demo from automated abuse, processing browser and network signals as described in Cloudflare's Turnstile Privacy Addendum and Privacy Policy.

What we don't do

We don't sell your data or use it for advertising, and we don't retain chat content beyond what's needed to operate the demo.

Contact

Questions? Reach Solver Collaborative at answerquestions.ai.
